ABC Technologies - Company Policy Document

Refund Policy:
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of purchase. Refund requests after 14 days require manager approval. All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor before processing.

Subscription Cancellation:
Customers may cancel their subscription at any time. Cancellation requests for annual plans must be approved by a supervisor if requested within the first 30 days of renewal.

Account Closure:
Account closure requests are permanent and require supervisor approval. Data is retained for 90 days before permanent deletion.

Compensation Requests:
Any compensation requests due to service outages must be escalated to management for approval.

Escalation Policy:
Requests that cannot be resolved by standard agents must be escalated to management.
